An Online GIS portal for School Information of Bara Tehsil of Prayagraj District


About Us

The sole purpose of this website is to show the changes in school faclities and other information from 2012-13 to 2017-18 in Shankargarh area of Bara Tehsil in Prayagraj District.

Anyone can access the website with use of mere internet and without any requiremnet of GIS tools.

The Side bar gives links for comparing specific type of information in two academic years (2012-13 and 2017-18) in vector data format. The Gallery Icon contains these information in Raster format (JPG or JPEG).
